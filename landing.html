SQL - Supply Chain Analysis

In this data project, various SQL queries were employed to analyze a dataset related to product sales. The project aimed to gain insights into product pricing, availability, sales, and revenue generation. Several SQL functions were utilized, including aggregation functions like AVG(), SUM(), MAX(), and MIN(), along with GROUP BY to organize data based on specific columns. Queries were structured to calculate average prices per product type, total revenue generated from sales, average product availability, and identify products with the highest and lowest availability. Additionally, the analysis included determining product types with the highest sales and examining the pricing ranges within product categories. These queries are essential for businesses seeking comprehensive insights into their product performance, pricing strategies, and sales trends.
